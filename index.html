Research Questions

Our work combines behavioral experiments, neural data, and computational theory to address the following key questions:

  • 1

    How do people understand and predict others’ actions and intentions?

  • 2

    How do self–other distinctions shape value computation and decision control?

  • 3

    How do cognitive and motivational systems modulate adaptive decision-making strategies?

  • 4

    How do these processes change across the lifespan and in subclinical populations?
