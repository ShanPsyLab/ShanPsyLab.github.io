Research Areas

We combine theoretical modeling with data analysis.

Neural Circuit Dynamics

We use dynamical systems theory to understand how populations of neurons coordinate to represent information. Our focus is on attractor dynamics in the prefrontal cortex.

Biologically Plausible AI

Developing learning algorithms that adhere to local synaptic plasticity rules, offering more energy-efficient alternatives to standard backpropagation.

Computer Vision & Perception

Analyzing the hierarchy of the visual ventral stream to improve object recognition robustness in convolutional neural networks.

Brain-Computer Interfaces

Decoding motor intent from neural population activity to drive prosthetic devices with higher degrees of freedom.
